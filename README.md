# Hi, I'm Adriano 👋

I'm a **Product Manager who builds**. I ship products in **FinTech risk** by day, and build **AI agents and personal-software tools** the rest of the time.

Most of my work lives where messy human problems meet technical systems: risk decisioning, digital health, and AI that's actually trustworthy. I care less about *talking* about products and more about *shipping* them — which is exactly why this GitHub exists.

I'm currently **PM on the Tech Risk team at [Qomodo](https://www.qomodo.me/)**, a FinTech (BNPL) scale-up — after 6+ years across digital health and venture capital.

## What I'm building 🛠️

- 🤖 **AI agents & copilots** — research agents, a personal digital twin, and risk assistants, mostly on Claude Code
- 🧠 **Thinking tools** — knowledge & life-management systems that help people reason, not just take notes
- 📊 **Risk & decisioning** — merchant underwriting, AML, and consumer risk (the day job)
- ✍️ **Writing** — a newsletter on product innovation in health tech, and (yes, really) poetry
